# Mentally Prepare

> An anonymous peer reset for students who feel emotionally disconnected, unseen, or overwhelmed.

Mentally Prepare is a startup app prototype that combines emotional pattern scanning, archetype-based insights, anonymous matching, and a guided 21-day journey to help users reflect, connect, and understand themselves more deeply.

---

## The Problem

Many students are surrounded by people but still feel alone.

They struggle with:
- Emotional disconnection
- Overthinking and self-doubt
- Fear of being misunderstood
- Social pressure to appear fine
- Hidden loneliness
- Difficulty expressing what they truly feel

Most student wellness products feel like habit trackers, generic journaling apps, motivational quote platforms, or clinical mental health tools.

**Mentally Prepare is built to feel different.**

---

## How It Works

The product experience works in 4 stages:

### 1. Emotional Scan
Users answer an 11-question reflective assessment that maps four psychological axes:
- **Rejection Sensitivity** — how strongly you react to perceived social rejection
- **Behavioural Avoidance** — how often you withdraw from connection opportunities
- **Vulnerability Threshold** — how willing you are to be emotionally open
- **Connection Readiness** — how prepared you are to build meaningful relationships

### 2. Archetype Reveal
Based on scan results, users receive one of four emotional connection archetypes:

| Archetype | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| 🌑 The Retreating Protector | Wants connection but locks the door before anyone gets close |
| 🌒 The Anxious Connector | Gives everything in relationships — it still doesn't feel enough |
| 🌓 The Invisible Performer | Everyone knows them, but nobody *knows* them |
| 🌔 The Drifting Disconnector | Connections start strong, then they quietly pull away |

### 3. Anonymous Matching
Users are paired with their **complementary opposite type** from a different college. The pairing creates productive tension — two people who connect differently, learning from each other.

### 4. 21-Day Guided Journey
Each night, both partners receive the same reflective prompt. They write independently, entries seal at midnight, and unseal together. On Day 21, both must consent to reveal identities — one "no" keeps it anonymous forever.

## Key Features

- **Social Architecture Scan** — 11-question emotional pattern assessment
- **Archetype-based insight** — personalized results with trait breakdowns
- **Anonymous match system** — opposite-type pairing across colleges
- **21-day prompt journey** — guided nightly writing with streak tracking
- **Midnight seal ritual** — entries lock at midnight, unseal together
- **Consent-based reveal** — both must say yes; zero rejection risk
- **Mood tracking** — nightly emotional check-in (🌑 Heavy → 🌕 Good)
- **Emotionally rich UI** — celestial dark theme with ambient animations
- **Progressive Web App** — installable, mobile-first experience
- **Export & share** — download entries, share archetype results

## Target Audience

- College students experiencing hidden loneliness
- Young adults who feel emotionally overwhelmed
- People who want real connection without performative social media
- Users who are curious about their emotional patterns

---

## Design Direction

The product is intentionally designed to feel:
- **Intimate** — like a private conversation, not a public feed
- **Reflective** — prompts that make you pause and think
- **Premium** — celestial aesthetic with rich typography and animations
- **Mysterious** — the anonymity creates anticipation
- **Emotionally safe** — consent-first, no rejection risk
- **Different** — this is not another wellness checkbox app
